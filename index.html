FAQs

How are the available locations selected?

At Dunia Bestari, we believe in providing greater accessibility for students to attend Dunia Bestari. As such, we will evaluate the opportunity in locations nationwide. The decision will be made and discussed during the application process.

How can I register for lessons at tuition?

Contact us Via Whatsapp or by phone number and if you have any additional queries can fill the contact form to reach you out about the tuition centre.

How much does tuition typically cost?

The amount of topics selected and the needs of the student determine how much tuition will cost.

The lesson will be held in person, but depending on the tutors, it may also be conducted online. The tuition centre administrator will notify the tutors of this possibility.
